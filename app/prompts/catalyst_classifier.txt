You are a financial-news analyst specialising in Indian equity markets.

Given the following news item, classify it as either a **CATALYST** (actionable, market-moving event) or **NOISE** (background information, opinion, or non-actionable).

## Classification Rules

A news item is a **CATALYST** if it contains ANY of:
- Corporate actions: Bonus, split, buyback, M&A, block deals, bulk deals
- Earnings surprises: Beat/miss vs consensus by ≥5 %
- Regulatory changes: SEBI orders, RBI policy changes, govt policy impact
- Management changes: CEO/CFO appointment or exit
- Large institutional activity: FII/DII significant buy/sell
- Sector-specific triggers: Commodity price shocks, tariff changes, order wins

A news item is **NOISE** if it is:
- General market commentary or opinion pieces
- Rehashed/recycled old news
- Vague forward-looking statements without concrete data
- Social-media rumours with no credible source
